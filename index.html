Research

  • The Human Inductive Bias Project - I am currently organising and running a research project on the intersection between active inference and machine learning, covering learning dynamics, fundamental research, and possible future architectures. The motivation is to apply these insights to AI safety, in particular looking at how and why capabilities are acquired and how generalisation occurs.
  • Harms from Increasingly Agentic Systems - A paper I was a co-author on, talking about the risks from AI systems we are yielding autonomy to.
  • AI Generated Multimodal Disinformation Networks - Conference presentation demonstrating some of the capabilities for AI to be used for complex disinformation narratives.
