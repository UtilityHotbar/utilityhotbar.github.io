About me

  • I have been coding since ~2018.
  • I program simulations, tools, toy compilers/languages, games, and multimedia projects/ARGs
  • My research interests include responsible uses for Large Language Models, AI safety/interpretability (e.g. occlusion sensitivity or risks from unscoped models with broad capabilities), Natural Language Processing (e.g. topic modelling, textual comprehension, and POS tagging) and the institutional incentives for developing AI technology.

Research

  • Harms from Increasingly Agentic Systems - A paper I was a co-author on, talking about the risks from AI systems we are yielding autonomy to.
  • AI Generated Multimodal Disinformation Networks - Conference presentation demonstrating some of the capabilities for AI to be used for complex disinformation narratives.
